## 1.0.0

- Initial release.
- `DatePatterns` — 20+ named format constants.
- `DateFormatter` — formatting, conversion, relative-time, and weekday utilities.
- `DateTimeClean` extension — `withoutMilliseconds()`.
- Full Vietnamese locale support via `intl`.
- Configurable labels for `convertDateVN` and `convertWeekdayVN`.
